About Me

Multidisciplinary developer Justin Soon

My story begins as a young enthusiast. I've been involved with computing ever since I can recall. I lived in the virtual world for most of my childhood. Revolved by technology, I started off producing films, with my friends. Fascinated by all the possibility I could share my story. It led me to become more in-depth with graphics, and utilizing them for my media. Using these skill sets had routed me to game servers. Creating promotional advert for their services. It sparked an interest of managing a small business at the age of fourteen. Putting together a community that included a staff and an extraordinary user experience. Dedicated to my service I was almost awake for all hours of the day to deal with users from multiple time zones. Creating a unique experience that lead me to create my own software for the players to use. Presently, I have a large skill set ranging from development to prototyping and I strive to reinvent the exceptional digital experiences.

Thanks for visiting me here.
